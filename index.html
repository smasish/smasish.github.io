About Me

I am Sarker Monojit Asish, an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science in Florida Polytechnic University. I completed my Ph.D. and M.Sc degrees in Computer Science at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. My research interests focused on virtual reality, human computer interactions (HCI), and Machine Learning.
