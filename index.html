About Me

I am Sarker Monojit Asish, an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science in Florida Polytechnic University. I completed my Ph.D. and M.Sc degrees in Computer Science at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette. My research interests focused on virtual reality, human computer interactions (HCI), Machine Learning and Deep Learning.

Our poster accepted in IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces (VR) on March, 2024, “Effectiveness of Visual Acuity Tests in VR vs the Real-World”.

Our TVCC paper accepted at the IEEE ISMAR 2024 Journal (IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics) on July, 2024. Paper titled "Classification of Internal and External Distractions in an Educational VR Environment Using Multimodal Features".
